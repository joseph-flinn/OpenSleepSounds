Text Colors

#F4E2BB — Radiant Readability

Against this darker green background (#002418), Soft Parchment stands out beautifully. The increased contrast makes the text pop cleanly without feeling harsh like a stark white would.

#EAD2AC — Editorial Khaki

This shade maintains its rich, warm undertones. It acts as an excellent, high-end focal color for headers and primary navigation states.

#D8B589 — Deep Camel Accent

Now that the background is darker, this Camel tone becomes an ideal utility color. It works perfectly for less prominent text, borders, or inactive tab elements. It also works for links. This example has text around it to show how the color is different.
