## Requirements
### Requirement: Audio controls centered on screen
The audio state and controls MUST be centered on the screen (both horizontally and vertically) regardless of device orientation or screen size.

#### Scenario: Center alignment on mobile
- **WHEN** the audio controls are displayed on a mobile device
- **THEN** the controls are centered horizontally and vertically on the screen

#### Scenario: Center alignment on desktop
- **WHEN** the audio controls are displayed on a desktop device
- **THEN** the controls are centered horizontally and vertically on the screen

#### Scenario: Responsive center alignment
- **WHEN** the screen size changes (orientation or dimensions)
- **THEN** the audio controls maintain their centered position
